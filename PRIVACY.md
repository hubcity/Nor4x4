# Nor4x4 Privacy Policy

## When installing this app you will be asked to grant two permissions

### Allow Nor4x4 to send you notifications?

Nor4x4 will use this permission to put an icon in the notification area of your watch to let you know that the Nor4x4 timers are running.  This is so you always have an indication that the timers are running, even if you switch to another app.

### Allow Nor4x4 to access heart rate?

Nor4x4 will use this permission to display your heart rate while the Nor4x4 timers are running.  Nor4x4 will continue to track your heart rate even if the app is put into the background.  When the timers have completed Nor4x4 will display your range of heart rates experienced during the session.  Nor4x4 will stop tracking your heart rate once the timers have completed.

## Your Data

Nor4x4 does not send or otherwise transmit any of your data to anyone.
